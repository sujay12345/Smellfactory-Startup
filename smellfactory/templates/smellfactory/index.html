What do we do?

Ever heard of smell training? Yes, you can gain your sense of smell back with smell training. We update you on how that works and how to do it.

We provide constant support so that you don't fall into the world of exacerbation.

We provide Forums for people who have been impacted by Anosmia/Parosmia to share their experiences through blogs and let people know how they've won this exasperating disease.

We also provide 24/7 chat support for members.

Parosmia can leave you trapped in a world of distorted scents.Yes, we notice that and we are there to help you with it by learning what to eat and on knowing the triggers.

We post frequent videos on dealing with that too.
